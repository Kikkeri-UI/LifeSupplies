Frequently Asked Questions

We build all of our formulations around powerful natural ingredients, from the teeth-whitening papaya enzymes in our toothpaste to the hair-nourishing orange extract in our shampoo and conditioner. When we can’t do the job with plants alone, we’ll use a safe synthetic. Just like the pairing of sweat-stopping synthetic salts and moisture-mopping hemp extracts in our bestselling antiperspirant.

Together with some of the UK’s finest designers, we’ve created a range of refillable bottles that are built to last. Depending on the product, the bases are either made of reinforced frosted glass or sturdy brushed aluminium. The removable lids are made from smooth stainless steel. Get a lifetime bottle in your first delivery, then refill forever.

Most of our refills come in recyclable paper-based cartons. We like them because they use up to 98% less plastic than regular bathroom bottles. Our cartons are kerbside recycled in most of the UK (and soon, all of the UK). Our toothpaste refill pouches are made from recycled plastic. Unlike our refill cartons, they’re not kerbside recycled. But don’t worry, we’ll give you a free returns envelope so you can send them back to us to be properly recycled.

Subscribe to a refill plan and take shopping for bathroom essentials off your to-do list – for good. Get a refillable bottle in your first order, then ongoing refills delivered on your schedule. We designed our default refill frequencies so that you don’t run out or have lots leftover, but you can easily change your frequency in your account. We’ll never lock you in – you can pause, edit or cancel your plan at any time. And when you choose a plan, you save 15% on your refillable bottle and all future refills.

By switching to one of our refillable bathroom essentials and refilling for a year, you save an average of 11 plastic bottles from landfill. The more of your bathroom essentials that you switch, the more plastic you save.
